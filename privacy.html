MenuPlan Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

MenuPlan ("the app") is a meal-planning app made by Stephen Brines. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. The short version: MenuPlan does not collect, track, or sell your personal information.

Information the app stores

MenuPlan stores the content you create — meal plans, recipes, meals, and grocery lists — on your device and in your own iCloud account using Apple's iCloud (CloudKit). This data is associated with your personal Apple ID and is not accessible to the developer. The app does not require you to create an account or provide your name, email, or other personal details to use it.

Family sharing

If you choose to share your meal plans with family, MenuPlan uses Apple's iCloud sharing to make that content available to the people you invite. Sharing happens entirely within Apple's iCloud infrastructure between you and the people you choose. The developer does not receive, store, or have access to this shared content.

Purchases

MenuPlan offers an optional in-app purchase (MenuPlan Premium) processed by Apple through the App Store. Payment and billing are handled entirely by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer does not receive your payment details.

Analytics and tracking

MenuPlan does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. The app does not track you across other apps or websites. Apple may provide the developer with aggregate, anonymized App Store statistics (such as download counts), which do not identify individual users.

Data deletion

Because your data lives in your own iCloud account and on your device, you can delete it at any time by removing meal plans within the app, or by deleting the app and its iCloud data from your device settings.

Children's privacy

MenuPlan is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
