Frequently asked

How does family sharing work?
Open Settings → Family Sharing → Share Meal Plans with Family and send the invite. The person you invite taps the link on their own device and accepts — your meal plans and recipes then sync to their phone through iCloud.

Do I need an account?
No. MenuPlan stores your data in your own iCloud account. There's no separate sign-up.

Is my data backed up?
Your meal plans sync through iCloud, so they're available across your devices and restored when you set up a new one.

What does MenuPlan Premium include?
Premium unlocks the full set of planning features as a one-time in-app purchase. You can restore it on your other devices anytime from Settings.
